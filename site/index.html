01 / About
— Yaira Soto, founder

Bilingual · Driven · Hardworking

From one client every few months to fully booked.

As the years have passed, I’ve grown as a nail tech — from one client every few months to almost every week fully booked. The journey hasn’t been easy, but I’ll forever be grateful for what it has taught me. Tears, blood, sweat, dedication, support, and faith brought me here. The moments I felt like giving up are the ones that feel most special now, because I made it through.

I pray for an amazing future as a nail tech and as I work toward becoming a nurse. To my parents who immigrated here to give me the American dream I get to live every day — los amo. To my sisters for supporting me unconditionally. And to Chinos, who let me give her ugly nails for years and never stopped encouraging me. I’d do it all over again in a heartbeat.
