TIER 1 — STRONGEST MATCHES FOR YAIRA'S VIBE
-------------------------------------------

1. BASE COAT NAIL SALON  (Honorable Mention)
   Awwwards page : https://www.awwwards.com/sites/base-coat-nail-salon
   Why it fits   : Soft warm-pink palette, big editorial photography
                   of hands, circular logo typography, confident salon
                   identity. This is closest to the direction we already
                   started for Yaira.
   Steal these   : color palette, hand photography style, type-on-curve
                   logo treatment.

2. SWITCH NAILS  (Nominee, Apr 2026)
   Awwwards page : https://www.awwwards.com/sites/switch-nails
   Why it fits   : E-commerce, but the way it presents press features
                   (Vogue, Elle, Plaza, Femina, Daisy) and the editorial
                   product photography is gold. Fashion-magazine energy.
   Steal these   : "as featured in" magazine logo strip, editorial
                   product/hand shots, clean fashion-house typography.

3. YUKIE NAIL & HEARTS HAIR NY  (Honorable Mention)
   Awwwards page : https://www.awwwards.com/sites/yukie-nail-and-hearts-hair-ny
   Why it fits   : Dark, cinematic photography with outline-only white
                   typography over moody portraits. Big NYC fashion vibe.
                   A more dramatic, edgier path if Yaira wants that.
   Steal these   : outline typography over photo, vertical side-bar
                   typography, dramatic mood.
